Request Account & Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your Festive Invite account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

How to Request Deletion:
  • Send an email to support@festiveinvite.com
  • Include the following information in your email:
    • Your first name
    • Your last name
    • The email address you used to log in (Google or Apple)
What happens next?
  • Our support team will verify your request and delete your account and all associated data from our servers.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.
  • If you have any questions, please contact us at support@festiveinvite.com.

Data Retention: All your account data, including contacts and event information, will be permanently deleted. We do not retain any personal data after your account is deleted.
