Privacy Policy for Festive Invite

Last updated: August 30, 2025

Contact Permission Notice: We use READ_CONTACTS permission only when you explicitly choose to import contacts for event invitations. Your contact data is not stored on our servers and is only used to help you easily invite friends and family to your events.

Information We Collect

Contact Information (Optional)

Festive Invite may access your device's contact list only when you:

  • Explicitly grant permission to access contacts
  • Choose to use the "Select from Contacts" feature when adding guests to events
  • Tap the "Import from Contacts" button in the guest management section

Important: Contact data is processed locally on your device and is NOT stored on our servers.

Account Information

  • Name and email address from Google or Apple Sign-In
  • Profile information from your authentication provider

Event Data

  • Events you create (name, date, location, description)
  • Guest lists for your events
  • RSVP responses from invited guests
  • Event templates and customizations

How We Use Your Information

Contact Data Usage

When you grant contact access, we use this information to:

  • Display your contacts in a searchable list for easy selection
  • Pre-fill guest information (name, phone, email) when adding invitees
  • Send event invitations via SMS or email to selected contacts
  • Provide a better user experience by eliminating manual data entry

Data Handling: Contact information is only accessed temporarily during the import process and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

General App Functionality

  • Create and manage your events
  • Send invitations to your guests
  • Track RSVP responses
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve app performance and features

Data Storage and Security

  • Contact Data: NOT stored on our servers - processed locally only
  • Event Data: Securely stored using industry-standard encryption
  • Account Data: Protected with secure authentication methods
  • Communications: All data transmission uses secure HTTPS protocols

Your Rights and Control

Contact Permissions

  • You can revoke contact access at any time through device settings
  • The app works fully without contact access - you can add guests manually
  • No contact data is retained if you revoke permission

Account Management

  • Delete your events and data at any time
  • Update your profile information
  • Request complete account deletion

Third-Party Services

We integrate with these services under their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Sign-In: Authentication only
  • Apple Sign-In: Authentication only
  • SMS/Email Services: For sending invitations (no data storage)

Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted in the app with an updated date.

  • Contact information is used solely to help you easily add guests to your event invitations
  • Contact data is processed locally on your device and is not stored on our servers
  • We only access contacts when you actively use the contact selection feature

Contact information accessed through the contact picker is not transmitted to or stored on our servers. All contact data remains on your device.
