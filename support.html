Festive Invite Support

Need Help? We're here to assist you with any questions or issues you may have with Festive Invite. Our support team is ready to help!

Contact Support

Send us an email and we'll get back to you as soon as possible:

support@festiveinvite.com

How to Get Help

When contacting support, please include the following information to help us assist you better:

  • Your name: First and last name
  • Email address: The email you used to sign up (Google or Apple)
  • Device information: iPhone/Android and version
  • Issue description: Detailed description of the problem
  • Steps to reproduce: What you were doing when the issue occurred

Common Support Topics

Account & Login Issues

Problems with signing in, account access, or authentication with Google/Apple accounts.

Event Management

Help with creating events, managing guest lists, sending invitations, or tracking RSVPs.

Contact Permissions

Questions about contact access, importing contacts, or managing contact permissions.

Technical Issues

App crashes, performance problems, or features not working as expected.

Privacy & Data

Questions about data privacy, account deletion, or how your information is used.

Response Time

We typically respond to support emails within 24-48 hours. For urgent issues, please mention "URGENT" in your email subject line.
